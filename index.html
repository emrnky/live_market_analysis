PoE Market Signal — Path of Exile 2 market signals

PoE Market Signal is a community-built tool that monitors live Path of Exile 2 trade data and runs it through a machine learning model to predict where item prices are heading. Each item gets a simple BUY, SELL, or HOLD signal so you can spot the best flipping opportunities before the rest of the market catches on.

Not affiliated with or endorsed by Grinding Gear Games. All signals are generated automatically and are for informational purposes only — not financial or trading advice.
