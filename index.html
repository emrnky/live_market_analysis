PoE Market Signal — Path of Exile 2 market signals

PoE Market Signal is a website built to snipe current Path of Exile 2 market values using a machine learning model that reads live trade data and predicts where the market is heading. We turn that prediction into a simple buy, sell, or hold signal for thousands of items, so you can find the best trade opportunities before the rest of the market catches on. PoE Market Signal is not affiliated with or endorsed by Grinding Gear Games — it's built by the community, for the community. Tables currently refresh every 24 hours. If this tool saves you time or currency, consider supporting the project.
